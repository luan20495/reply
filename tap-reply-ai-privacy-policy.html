Privacy Policy

App: Tap Reply AI
Effective date: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tap Reply AI (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the Android application Tap Reply AI.

1. What Tap Reply AI does

Tap Reply AI is an Android productivity app that helps users automate message reply actions. The App may use Android Accessibility Service and overlay features to support user-requested automation and quick-reply workflows on the device.

2. Information we collect

We do not intentionally collect, store, or sell personal data on our servers.

  • App settings you configure, such as reply text and feature preferences.
  • On-device diagnostic logs for troubleshooting and testing.
  • Temporary on-screen content needed to perform user-requested actions on your device.

3. Accessibility Service use

Tap Reply AI may use Android Accessibility Service solely to help users perform automation features they explicitly enable, such as detecting chat screens, identifying target message areas, and sending configured replies.

Accessibility data is used only for the App’s core functionality on your device. We do not use Accessibility Service to collect data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

4. Overlay permission use

The App may request permission to display content over other apps in order to show a quick-reply control panel or overlay interface. This permission is used only for the functionality requested by the user.

5. How your data is processed

All core automation processing is intended to occur locally on the device unless a future version of the App clearly states otherwise. At this time, we do not provide a cloud account system and do not upload personal conversations to our servers as part of normal use.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers.

7. Data retention

Any settings or logs stored by the App remain on your device unless you remove them by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce unnecessary data access and keep processing limited to the features required for the App to function. However, no method of software operation can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s privacy

Tap Reply AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Your choices

  • You can disable Accessibility Service at any time in Android Settings.
  • You can revoke overlay permission at any time in Android Settings.
  • You can delete app data or uninstall the App at any time.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
